Privacy Policy

  • ConvertAll and its developer take your privacy very seriously.
  • All data that users input is stored locally, on the user's own device.
  • ConvertAll uses no third-party analytics or advertising frameworks.
  • If you email the developer for support or other feedback, the emails will be retained for quality assurance purposes. The email addresses will be used only to reply to the concerns or suggestions raised and will never be used for any other purpose.
  • Other than as noted above, ConvertAll does not collect, transmit, distribute or sell your data.
